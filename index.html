About

Pastor Charles: The Christian Life Coach.

Get to Know Me

Charles Owidi Omollo is an experienced church minister and Christian life coach. He has specialized coaching in marriages and dating relationships and works with both individuals and groups.

Vision Statement:

To inspire and aluminate the lives of those God brings my way

Education

Light University (Masters in Mental Health Life Coaching)

Certification

The American Association of Christian Counselors.
AACC proudly recognizes the diversity within the helping relationship and provides membership opportunities in three separate categories: Professionals include psychiatrists, psychologists, social workers, licensed counselors, and others who have advanced training in one of the mental health professions.

Bio, History and Ministry

As a seasoned minister, he strives to help God’s children live life to the full. He is also passionate about taking care of people’s needs and helping them create healthy relationships. In addition to being an ordained minister of God, he also does premarital classes for couples planning to get married. He is an alumnus of Light University where he became a certified mental health practitioner and Christian life coaching. He is also a member of the American association of Christian counsellors. He currently is working with HWPL which is an international peace NGO in consultative status with UN ECOSOC and UN DGC as a consultant. He is the founder of New Life Health Opinion (NLHO), which is a Christian health-based organization with an aim of helping to facilitate options in healthy living.
